# Problem Statement

## Overview

TDA is a centralized local sports hub application focused on Puerto Rico sports. The app fills a gap in the local sports community because, currently, there is no major local sports app dedicated to organizing Puerto Rico sports content in one place.

Puerto Rico has a large and passionate sports fan base, especially in sports such as baseball, basketball, boxing, combat sports, and volleyball. Even though local leagues and athletes continue to grow, their digital presence is still limited compared to larger international leagues like the NBA or MLB.

The main goal of TDA is to give local sports fans a single place where they can find scores, schedules, team information, news, and updates related to Puerto Rico sports.

---

## Main Problem

The main problem TDA solves is the lack of a centralized sports platform for local Puerto Rico sports.

Right now, local sports information is spread across different sources such as social media pages, news websites, team accounts, league pages, and older posts. This makes it difficult for users to quickly find information about their favorite teams, leagues, athletes, or recent game results.

For many fans, finding a final score, schedule, stat, or update requires searching through multiple platforms. This is inefficient and can discourage users from following local sports as closely as they would like.

---

## Why the App is Needed

Puerto Rico has a strong sports culture. Many families on the island have at least one regular sports fan, and local sports are an important part of the community.

However, local leagues do not have the same level of visibility or mobile presence as larger international leagues. Apps like ESPN, CBS Sports, Bleacher Report, and The Athletic provide strong coverage for major leagues, but they do not focus enough on Puerto Rico’s local sports scene.

TDA is needed because local sports fans deserve a platform built specifically for them.

---

## Target Audience

The main target audience is local sports fans in Puerto Rico.

More specifically, TDA focuses on the following groups:

### Casual Sports Fans

Casual sports fans want a simple way to check final scores, schedules, and updates without having to search across multiple websites or social media pages.

These users may not follow every game live, but they still want to stay informed about their favorite teams and leagues.

### Daily Sports Enthusiasts

Daily sports enthusiasts want quick access to scores, schedules, previous game results, and stats.

These users are more active and may follow multiple sports or teams. They may currently have trouble finding older stats or game information because local sports content is often spread across social media posts or different websites.

### People Involved in Sports

This includes athletes, coaches, team staff, reporters, photographers, editors, and content creators.

These users can benefit from the app because it can provide more visibility for athletes, teams, and local leagues. It can also help reporters, photographers, and content creators reach more fans through a centralized sports platform.

---

## Why Current Apps or Platforms Do Not Fully Solve This Problem

Current sports apps do not fully solve this problem because they mainly focus on major international leagues and larger sports markets.

Apps like ESPN, CBS Sports, Bleacher Report, and The Athletic are useful for following leagues like the NBA, MLB, NFL, and other major sports organizations. However, they do not provide consistent or detailed coverage of Puerto Rico local sports leagues, teams, and athletes.

Social media pages and local news websites help provide coverage, but they are not built like sports apps. They are harder to search, less organized, and usually do not provide a complete experience for scores, schedules, teams, stats, and notifications.

Because of this, there is still a clear gap in the Puerto Rico sports app market.

---

## What Makes TDA Different

TDA is different because it is built specifically for local sports in Puerto Rico.

Instead of treating Puerto Rico sports as a small side topic, TDA makes local leagues, teams, athletes, and fans the main focus of the application.

The app is designed to help grow the visibility of local sports by organizing important sports information in one place. This includes scores, schedules, team pages, news, and future features such as athlete profiles, rankings, and live updates.

TDA is not only a sports app. It is a local sports hub made for the Puerto Rico sports community.

---

## Market Gap

There is a wide gap in the sports app market, especially in Puerto Rico.

Currently, there is no well-known major sports app dedicated to the island’s local sports scene. This creates an opportunity to build a platform that serves local fans, athletes, teams, leagues, and sports media in a more focused way.

TDA can help fill this untapped space by giving Puerto Rico sports a stronger digital presence and making local sports content easier to access.

---

## Summary

TDA solves the problem of scattered and difficult-to-find Puerto Rico sports information.

The app is needed because local sports fans currently do not have one centralized platform where they can easily follow teams, check scores, view schedules, read updates, and stay connected with the local sports community.

By focusing on Puerto Rico sports first, TDA can provide a better experience for local fans and help increase the visibility of local leagues, athletes, and sports content.
