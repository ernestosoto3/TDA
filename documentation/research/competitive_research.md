# Sports Apps Competitive Research

## Overview

This research analyzes existing sports apps, platforms, and Puerto Rico sports media pages to understand their strengths, weaknesses, and possible areas of improvement.

The main areas reviewed are:

- **Scores**
- **Schedules**
- **News**
- **Teams**
- **Personalization**
- **Notifications**
- **UI/UX**
- **Local sports coverage**

## Apps and Platforms Researched

- Apple Sports
- FOX Sports
- ESPN
- Bleacher Report
- theScore
- The Athletic
- Puerto Rico Sports Nation
- El Nuevo Día Deportes

---

## 1. Apple Sports

### Useful Features

- Provides fast live scores.
- Supports Live Activities on the iPhone lock screen and Dynamic Island.
- Does not include ads.
- Has a simple scoreboard-focused experience.
- Syncs favorite teams with Apple’s **My Sports** ecosystem.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- The UI can be difficult to navigate.
- Not available in all countries.
- Missing basic sport-specific details for some leagues.
- Does not include full team rosters.
- Does not include injury reports.
- Does not provide full schedules for every team or league.
- Does not include video highlights inside the app.
- News is not directly available inside the app.

### Scores

Apple Sports provides fast real-time scores in a clean scoreboard format. Games are separated into views such as **yesterday**, **today**, and **upcoming**.

### Schedules

Schedules are easy to access, but the app is limited compared to larger sports platforms. It focuses more on current and upcoming games than deeper team schedule details.

### News

Apple Sports does not include articles directly inside the app. Instead, it redirects users to Apple News for articles and Apple TV for video or live game content.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can favorite teams, and those favorites sync across Apple services. However, team pages are limited and do not include enough detailed information.

### Notifications

Apple Sports avoids heavy push notifications. Its biggest notification-related feature is **Live Activities**, which allows users to track live games from the lock screen or Dynamic Island.

### Opportunities for This App

- Add full team rosters.
- Add injury reports.
- Add more complete team schedules.
- Add video highlights directly inside the app.
- Add widgets.
- Add more control over notification frequency.
- Add an optional delay for live updates to avoid spoilers.
- Improve navigation and overall UI clarity.

## 2. FOX Sports

### Useful Features

- Includes a live sports feed.
- Supports live TV provider streaming.
- Provides video highlights and studio show content.
- Allows users to follow teams and receive alerts.
- Supports features like key plays, instant rewind, and multiview streaming.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- Video and stream loading can be unreliable.
- Streaming quality is sometimes inconsistent.
- The app has a heavy emphasis on ads.
- The app has a heavy emphasis on betting content.
- Some users receive too many uninteresting notifications.
- Some users experience missing or inconsistent notifications.
- Written content is not always as in-depth as other sports platforms.

### Scores

FOX Sports provides real-time scores, play-by-play updates, and betting lines such as spreads and money lines.

### Schedules

The app provides schedules for different leagues and events. It also includes tournament dashboards and game panels.

### News

FOX Sports focuses heavily on video content, live shows, highlights, and sports media. Written articles are available, but video is one of the main strengths of the platform.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can follow teams, players, and leagues. The app uses those selections to build a personalized feed.

### Notifications

FOX Sports provides alerts for game starts, scoring plays, and important updates. It also supports Live Activities for real-time score tracking.

### Opportunities for This App

- Reduce the amount of ads.
- Improve stream reliability.
- Improve video loading speed.
- Reduce the emphasis on betting content.
- Make notifications more relevant.
- Provide deeper written content and team stories.
- Add end-of-quarter, end-of-inning, or end-of-period summaries.

## 3. ESPN

### Useful Features

- Provides a large amount of sports articles.
- Includes short game recaps and notes.
- Offers video highlights.
- Covers many sports, leagues, and teams.
- Includes stats, fantasy sports information, and ESPN+ integration.
- Provides detailed game pages with stats and play-by-play information.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- The app can feel disorganized.
- Content is ad-heavy.
- Some highlights have long ads before short videos.
- The app can lag or feel unreliable.
- Notifications sometimes open the home page instead of the specific article or game update.

### Scores

ESPN provides a very complete scoring system. It includes live scores, play-by-play, team stats, player stats, and win probability.

### Schedules

ESPN has strong schedule coverage across many sports and leagues. Users can view past, current, and upcoming games.

### News

ESPN is strong in sports news. It includes articles, videos, highlights, analysis, and ESPN+ content. However, the amount of content can make the app feel crowded.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can follow favorite teams and leagues. ESPN uses this information to create a personalized Favorites section and score feed.

### Notifications

ESPN offers many notification options, including breaking news, game updates, play-by-play alerts, and final score alerts. However, notifications can feel aggressive or too frequent.

### Opportunities for This App

- Improve organization and navigation.
- Reduce the amount of ads.
- Avoid long ads before short highlights.
- Improve app speed and reliability.
- Fix notification routing so alerts open the correct article, game, or update.
- Give users more control over notification frequency.
- Make the personalized feed cleaner and more relevant.

## 4. Bleacher Report

### Useful Features

- Known for fast sports notifications.
- Strong focus on sports culture, rumors, and trending stories.
- Allows users to follow teams and leagues.
- Includes short-form videos and highlights.
- Offers community-style content and fan discussion.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- Comments are not as useful as they used to be.
- The app pushes too much short video content.
- Some users feel the app moved away from being a community-driven sports hub.
- News can feel more focused on viral content than deep reporting.

### Scores

Bleacher Report includes live scoreboards, score updates, stats, betting lines, and highlights for major sports.

### Schedules

The app provides game schedules for major leagues and teams. Its schedule experience is useful but not its main focus.

### News

Bleacher Report focuses on sports rumors, trending topics, short videos, internet culture, and quick updates.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can select favorite teams and leagues. These selections help personalize the homepage and content feed.

### Notifications

Bleacher Report is known for quick alerts, especially for breaking news, trade rumors, scoring plays, and major sports updates.

### Opportunities for This App

- Improve the comment section.
- Reduce low-value short video content.
- Bring back a stronger community-driven experience.
- Improve content quality beyond viral stories.
- Give users more control over the type of alerts they receive.
- Create better moderation tools for community discussions.

## 5. theScore

### Useful Features

- Highly customizable for followed teams.
- Easy to navigate.
- Large catalog of sports and leagues.
- Clean UI with fewer distractions.
- Provides fast scores and detailed player information.
- Strong notification customization.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- Chat sections can include spam.
- Chat sections can include bots.
- Large ads appear in the middle of the app.
- Betting integration can feel too present for some users.

### Scores

theScore is very strong with fast live scores and detailed game pages. It includes player box scores, game trackers, fantasy-related stats, and betting information.

### Schedules

Schedules are easy to find and organized by sport, league, and team.

### News

theScore includes quick sports news, clips, highlights, short videos, and community discussion areas.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can favorite teams, leagues, and players. The app creates a personalized feed based on these selections.

### Notifications

theScore provides detailed notification controls. Users can receive alerts for specific teams, players, scoring plays, game starts, and final scores.

### Opportunities for This App

- Improve chat moderation.
- Reduce spam and bots in chats.
- Make ads less intrusive.
- Reduce the pressure of betting-related content.
- Keep improving the clean navigation experience.
- Give users better control over what appears in their feed.

## 6. The Athletic

### Useful Features

- Provides in-depth information on professional teams.
- Strong journalism and long-form articles.
- Well-designed app.
- Good team-specific reporting.
- Includes analysis, columns, podcasts, and insider content.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- The algorithm can show irrelevant news before followed topics.
- Some users report too many ads or promoted content.
- Content is more focused on premium journalism than live score tracking.
- Less useful for users who mainly want quick updates.

### Scores

The Athletic includes clean scoreboards, standings, and game tracking. However, live scores are not the main focus of the app.

### Schedules

Schedules are available, but they are not as central to the experience as articles and analysis.

### News

The Athletic is strongest in news and analysis. It focuses on long-form articles, beat reporting, podcasts, and insider coverage.

### Teams and Personalization

Users can follow specific teams and leagues. The app builds a feed around those selections, but users may still see content they are not interested in.

### Notifications

Notifications usually focus on major stories, breaking news, trade updates, and important game results.

### Opportunities for This App

- Improve the algorithm so followed topics appear first.
- Reduce irrelevant content in the user feed.
- Reduce unwanted ads or promoted content.
- Improve quick-update features for users who do not want long articles.
- Make scores and schedules more central for users who want live game information.
- Give users more control over feed preferences.

## 7. Puerto Rico Sports Nation

### Useful Features

- Provides important Puerto Rico sports content.
- Covers different Puerto Rico sports and athletes.
- Strong community engagement through social media.
- Includes local updates that bigger sports apps may ignore.
- Content is usually active and relevant to Puerto Rico sports fans.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- Users cannot personalize the content.
- It is harder to find specific information because it is a Facebook page.
- Content may be harder to verify if sources are not always clear.
- Information can get lost in the social media feed.
- The platform is not very scalable as a long-term sports app experience.

### Scores

Scores are usually shared through posts, graphics, screenshots, or text updates. The information can be useful, but it is not organized like a sports app.

### Schedules

Schedules may be posted as graphics or updates, but they are not always easy to search or filter by team, sport, or league.

### News

News is shared through social media posts, videos, rumors, comments, and community discussions.

### Teams and Personalization

The page is community-driven and focused on Puerto Rico sports. However, users cannot follow specific leagues, teams, or players inside the page itself.

### Notifications

Notifications depend on Facebook. Users can receive alerts for new posts, live videos, and comments, but there are no native live score alerts or lock screen updates.

### Opportunities for This Platform

- Organize content by sport, league, team, and player.
- Make scores and schedules easier to search.
- Improve source validation for news and updates.
- Create a more scalable structure outside of a single social media feed.
- Post updates more frequently during live sporting events.
- Allow users to follow specific sports, teams, or leagues.

## 8. El Nuevo Día Deportes

### Useful Features

- Provides daily sports updates.
- Balances local and international sports coverage.
- Offers professional journalism and written articles.
- Covers important Puerto Rico athletes and sports stories.
- Includes interviews, opinion pieces, and sports reports.

### Problems, Flaws, or User Complaints

- It is hard to find older sports information quickly.
- Users cannot customize the sports news they see.
- If a user’s team is not covered that day, there may be no relevant content for them.
- The platform works more like a newspaper than a personalized sports app.
- Live scores and schedules are not the main focus.

### Scores

Scores are usually presented through articles, summaries, graphics, or daily updates. The platform does not focus on real-time scoring.

### Schedules

Schedules are not as interactive or personalized as in sports apps. They are usually part of articles or general sports coverage.

### News

El Nuevo Día Deportes is strong in regional sports journalism. It includes articles, interviews, opinion pieces, videos, and coverage of Puerto Rico athletes.

### Teams and Personalization

The website is organized by general sports categories instead of personalized team feeds. Users cannot easily follow specific teams, players, or leagues.

### Notifications

Notifications are usually general news alerts or daily updates. The platform does not focus on real-time sports notifications or Live Activities.

### Opportunities for This Platform

- Add better search for older sports content.
- Add personalization by sport, league, team, or player.
- Improve same-day and live sports updates.
- Add more interactive scores and schedules.
- Create dedicated pages for popular Puerto Rico leagues and teams.
- Make local sports coverage easier to filter and follow.

# Overall Findings

## Common Useful Features

- Fast live scores
- Favorite team tracking
- Personalized feeds
- Game schedules
- Player stats
- Video highlights
- Breaking news alerts
- Lock screen or Live Activity score tracking
- Team-specific pages
- Local or community-driven content

## Common Problems

- Too many ads
- Too many notifications
- Notifications that are not relevant
- Poor notification routing
- Disorganized UI
- Too much betting content
- Lack of local sports coverage
- Limited personalization
- Spam or bots in community sections
- Missing team rosters, injury reports, or player information
- Content feeds showing topics the user does not follow

## General Research Takeaways

- Sports apps need to balance speed, accuracy, and organization.
- Users want fast scores, but they also want control over notifications.
- Too many ads can damage the user experience.
- Betting content should not take over the main experience.
- Personalization is important, but it needs to respect what the user actually follows.
- Local sports coverage is still weak in many major sports apps.
- Social media pages are useful for local sports, but they are hard to search and organize.
- A strong sports platform should make scores, schedules, teams, players, news, and notifications easy to access.
